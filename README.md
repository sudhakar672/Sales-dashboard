## 📋 Sheet Descriptions

### ⚡ Executive Dashboard
- KPI banner cards: Total Revenue, Target, Achievement %, Net Profit, Units Sold, Expenses, Profit Margin
- Monthly performance summary table with conditional color-scale formatting
- Regional summary table
- Monthly Revenue Trend line chart

### 📅 Monthly Analysis
- Full 12-month breakdown with MoM Growth %
- Conditional formatting on Achievement % column
- **Charts:**
  - Monthly Revenue vs Target (Clustered Bar)
  - Monthly Net Profit Trend (Line)
  - Month-over-Month Growth % (Column)

### 🌐 Regional Analysis
- Performance breakdown: North | South | East | West
- Achievement %, Units Sold, Expenses, Net Profit per region
- **Charts:**
  - Revenue Share by Region (Pie)
  - Revenue vs Target by Region (Clustered Bar)
  - Net Profit by Region (Horizontal Bar)

### 📦 Product Analysis
- Product A | B | C | D comparison
- Avg Unit Price, Expenses, Profit Margin %
- **Charts:**
  - Revenue Share by Product (Pie)
  - Revenue vs Net Profit by Product (Clustered Bar)
  - Profit Margin % by Product (Horizontal Bar)

### 🏆 Rep Leaderboard
- All 8 sales representatives ranked by Total Revenue
- 🥇 Gold / 🥈 Silver / 🥉 Bronze medal highlights
- Data-bar conditional formatting on Revenue column
- **Charts:**
  - Revenue & Profit by Sales Rep (Horizontal Bar)
  - Target Achievement % by Rep (Horizontal Bar)

### 📋 Raw Data
- Clean, formatted source data (384 rows × 11 columns)
- Alternating row styles with ₹ number formatting
